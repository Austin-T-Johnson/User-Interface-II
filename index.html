About Me

Hello, i'm Austin! I have been working in the live entertainment

industry for the past 8 years as a audio engineer & VJ. What's a

the graphics for artists during a concert! As much as I love my

job, the hours are very demanding and you are away from home

more often than not. Since the pandemic hit, I really looked into

other avenues for work, and the one that kept popping up was

coding! I am very excited to start this new chapter and can't

wait to really begin!
